What is the pomodoro technique?

The pomodoro technique is a useful strategy for working created by Francesco Cirillo about thirty years ago. The strategy consists of 25 minute intervals of working with 5 minute breaks in between. A longer break is taken after the fourth interval of work. Inspired by the tomato-shaped timer Cirillo had, each interval is called a pomodoro, Italian for the red vegetable.
